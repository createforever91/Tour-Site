Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

Haven Cartagena Tours respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how customers can contact us about privacy requests.

Information we collect

  • Contact details such as name, email address, phone number, and WhatsApp number.
  • Booking details such as travel date, group size, hotel or pickup area, selected experience, and special requests.
  • Payment and transaction details processed through our payment providers. We do not intentionally store full card numbers on our website.
  • Messages, support requests, and communications sent through WhatsApp, email, website forms, or other channels.
  • Basic website analytics such as device, browser, referral, and page activity when analytics tools are used.

How we use information

We use customer information to confirm availability, process bookings, collect payment, coordinate tours with providers, provide customer support, send booking updates, respond to questions, improve our services, prevent fraud, and comply with legal or payment processor requirements.

Sharing information

We may share only the information needed to fulfill your booking with local providers such as guides, boat operators, drivers, beach clubs, activity providers, venues, customer support tools, payment processors, and professional advisors. We do not sell customer personal information.

Payment information

Payments may be handled by third-party payment processors. Those processors may collect and process payment details under their own privacy and security practices.

Data retention

We retain booking, support, and transaction information as long as needed to provide services, resolve disputes, meet tax or accounting obligations, prevent fraud, and comply with legal requirements.

Your choices

You may contact us to request access, correction, deletion, or limitation of your personal information, subject to legal, accounting, security, and booking-related requirements.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact info@haventoursctg.com.
