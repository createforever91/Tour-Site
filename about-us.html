About Us

Local Cartagena travel planning, made clear.

Paradise Cartagena Tours helps travelers discover and book trusted tours, boats, beach clubs, and custom group experiences in Cartagena, Colombia.

What we do

We operate as a tour and travel booking agency. Some experiences may be coordinated directly by our team, while other tours, boats, transportation, beach clubs, guides, and activities may be fulfilled by trusted independent local providers. We help customers compare options, understand pricing, confirm availability, and receive support around their booking.

How bookings work

Customers choose an experience on our website or request a custom itinerary. Our team confirms date, group size, pickup details, live availability, included services, excluded costs, provider rules, and the final price before payment when confirmation is needed.

Pricing and payment timing

Tour and boat prices are shown on our listing pages where available. Many bookings are future-dated, meaning customers may pay before the tour date to secure a reservation. Custom private trips, large groups, holiday dates, and provider-specific experiences may require a deposit or full prepayment.

Customer support

We provide customer support by WhatsApp and email before and after purchase. If a provider change, weather issue, cancellation, or schedule adjustment affects a booking, our team helps coordinate the next steps based on the terms for that experience.
