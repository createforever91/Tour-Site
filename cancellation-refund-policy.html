Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

This policy explains how cancellations, refunds, rescheduling, deposits, and no-shows are handled for Haven Cartagena Tours bookings.

Standard cancellation window

  • Cancellations made at least 48 hours before the scheduled experience may be eligible for a full or partial refund, depending on the provider and experience type.
  • Cancellations made less than 48 hours before the scheduled experience may be non-refundable.
  • Same-day cancellations, late arrivals, and no-shows are generally non-refundable.

Provider-specific rules

Private boats, beach clubs, holiday dates, peak-season bookings, large groups, special events, custom itineraries, and third-party provider reservations may have stricter cancellation terms. Those terms will control when they are disclosed before payment or shown in the booking confirmation.

Weather and safety cancellations

If an experience cannot safely operate because of weather, sea conditions, port restrictions, or provider safety decisions, we will help coordinate one of the following options when available: rescheduling, an alternative experience, credit toward a future booking, or a refund according to provider rules.

Deposits

Deposits may be required to reserve future-dated tours, boats, beach clubs, and custom experiences. Deposits may be non-refundable if the provider has already reserved inventory, staff, transport, boat crew, or venue space for your group.

Refund processing

Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method when possible. Bank, card network, and payment processor timing may vary after a refund is issued. Processing fees, currency conversion differences, or provider fees may not always be refundable.

How to request a cancellation or refund

Email info@haventoursctg.com or contact us by WhatsApp with your name, booking date, experience name, receipt or payment reference, and reason for cancellation. We will review your request and respond with the available options.
